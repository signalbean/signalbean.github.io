No "Hello"

Please don't just say hello in chat

Imagine calling someone, saying "hello!" then immediately putting them on hold... That's what you're doing.

❌ Don't Do This

hi
... 4 minutes later ...
...?
what time was that thing again?
oh - 3:30

People who do this are trying to be polite, but it's counter-productive. Typing is slower than talking. Despite best intentions, you're actually just making the other person wait for you to phrase your question, which is lost productivity.

The same goes for "u there?", "quick question...", or "ping".

✅ Instead, Try This

Hiya! What time was that thing?
hey, 3:30
Thanks!

Just ask the question! If you feel it's a bit brusque, you can still add pleasantries. The important thing is getting to the point.

This also allows for **asynchronous communication**. If the other person is away, they can still answer your question when they return, instead of just staring at a "Hello" and wondering what they missed. When done right - everyone's happy! 🎉
